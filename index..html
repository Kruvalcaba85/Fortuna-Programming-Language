About Fortuna

Fortuna is the programming language for those that have too much money! Every action has a cost and reward. Will you be lucky and output your entire program or will you go bankrupt?

Features

  • Pillars, Instruments, Wheels
  • Pillars: Essential Functions
  • Instruments: Outputting Functions
  • Weels: The Money Makers.
  • Also has options for lawful calls or multiple blinds.
  • Simple interface and syntax to get moving quickly.
